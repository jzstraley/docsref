    • InoDilators
      • Dobutamine
      • Target: ABBBB
      • 2-20 mcg/kg/min
      • High effect on SVR, BP
      • Milrinone
      • Target: cAMP
        • Decreases cAMP degradation
      • 375-0.75 mcg/kg/min SE: Hypotension, increased risk of Arrhythmias
      • Isoproterenol
      • Target: BBBB
      • 0-10 mcg/min
    • Pure Vasopressors
      • Vasopressin
      • Target: V1 and V2
      • 01-0.06 U/min
      • Phenylephrine
      • Target: AAAA
      • 40-180 mcg/min
    • InoPressors
      • Low effect on HR/Inotropy/Ectopy
      • High effect on SVR, BP
      • Norepinephrine (Levophed)
      • Target: AAAB
      • 0-40 mcg/min
      • Epinephrine
      • Target: ABBB
      • 0-20 mcg/min
      • Dopamine
      • Low
        • Target: Dopa-R
        • 1-4 mcg/kg/min
      • Medium
        • Target: ABBBD
        • 4-10 mcg/kg/min
      • High
        • Target: AAABD
        • 10-20 mcg/kg/min
